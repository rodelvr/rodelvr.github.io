Hi, I'm Rodel.

I was born and raised in the Netherlands. I am half Dutch and half Filipino (Pinoy!). My Dutch roots make me love the typical Dutch cuisines like zuurkool and Dutch snacks like stroopwafels. On the other hand I love being in tropical countries, visiting cultural places and be challenged by adventures. Currently I am happily living closeby Amsterdam.

Currently, I am working as a Data Scientist at Adyen. Adyen is known for building a modern payment infrastructure which is directly connected to card networks and local payment methods across the world, allowing for unified commerce and providing shopper data insights to merchants. The Adyen platform enables merchants, world-wide, to accept payments in a single system, enabling revenue growth online, on mobile devices and at the point of sale. My main responsibilities include but are not limited to developing machine learning algorithms, performing data engineering and doing (big) data analysis. This can be in the context of risk management, product design or improving authorization rates.

My career started as a Data Scientist at ING in the Netherlands. ING is the biggest Bank in the Netherlands and is regarded as an innovative company, usually being one of the first adopters of new technological advances in the banking sector. I have mainly worked on implementing machine learning models for risk management. The machine learning models focussed on predictive analytics for client behaviour and for performing client segmentation. Examples of this are prediction of mortgage prepayments, savings balances and segmentation of mortgage clients based on prepayment behaviour.
