Hi, I'm Rodel.

Currently, I am working as Founding Data Engineer at Solvimon. Solvimon is a next-generation billing platform designed to help fast growing businesses manage their billing and pricing operations flawlessly. With Solvimon, businesses can say goodbye to revenue leakage, billing errors, and manual processes that slow down growth. At Solvimon, I am responsible for everything data related, from setting up the data infrastructure to creating valuable insights for platforms.

My career started as a Data Scientist at ING Bank in the Netherlands. There I worked on implementing, improving and validating machine learning models for risk management. The projects focused on predictive analytics for client behaviour in mortgages and saving accounts using machine learning models. Examples of this are prediction of mortgage prepayments, savings balances and segmentation of mortgage clients based on prepayment behaviour.

After ING, I worked for 5 years at Adyen. At Adyen I have worked on building their data infrastructure and data products. Among others, I led the initiative that resulted in the first end-to-end machine learning model in production. This helped boost revenue for Adyen's by hundreds of millions of euros. At Adyen I have obtained broad knowledge on usage and implementation of business intelligence applications, data pipelines, data platform engineering and machine learning engineering.
