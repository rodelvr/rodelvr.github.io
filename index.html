Hi, I'm Rodel.

Welcome to my personal website.

Currently, I am working as a Tech Lead (Technical Lead) and Data Scientist at Adyen. In which I am responsible for leading a multidisciplinary team of Data Scientists and Software Engineers that aim to produce end-to-end Machine Learning based solutions. Adyen is known for building a modern payment infrastructure which is directly connected to card networks and local payment methods across the world, allowing for unified commerce and providing shopper data insights to merchants. The Adyen platform enables merchants, world-wide, to accept payments in a single system, enabling revenue growth online, on mobile devices and at the point of sale.

Examples of achieved projectrs:
  • A reinforcement learning framework to optimize the payment conversion rate of payments packaged in a product called RevenueAccelerate.
  • A machine learning based solution for automatically recovering subscription based payments called AutoRescue.
  • Establishing and maintaining data pre-processing and data pipelines (ETL) using Apache Spark and Apache Airflow.

My career started as a Data Scientist at ING Bank in the Netherlands. I have mainly worked on implementing machine learning models for risk management. The machine learning models focussed on predictive analytics for client behaviour and for performing client segmentation. Examples of this are prediction of mortgage prepayments, savings balances and segmentation of mortgage clients based on prepayment behaviour.
